Florence + The Machine

Oh, Miss Welch and the band have been around longer than my adoration for pizza. But it wasn't until a particularly trying situation while navigating independent life in the Indianapolis suburb, Westfield, when "Shake it Out" first landed my radar. At the time, I'd been dealing with a horrible case of fleas from the first dog I've ever loved. Something about her vocal range hits all the right spots for me.
