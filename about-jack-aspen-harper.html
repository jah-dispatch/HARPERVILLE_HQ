About Jack Aspen Harper


Jack Aspen Harper is a nonbinary author, creative mythmaker, and the curator behind Harperville™ — a digital sanctuary where narrative repair, emotional weather reports, and mythic archives converge. Their work spans MM romance, LGBTQ+ fiction, and the shimmering edges of digital storytelling, blending heart, humor, and cultural commentary into every corner of their universe.

Beyond the page, Jack is The Ozian Ambassador for The Emerald City, a devoted student of Ozian lore and Wicked fandom, and a lifelong Swiftie with a soft spot for pop‑culture mythmaking. Their creative toolkit includes book cover design, graphic design, cosmetics, skincare, fashion, and brand management — a true Jack‑of‑all‑trades whose artistry moves fluidly between mediums.

Jack also advocates for mental health and disability awareness, drawing from lived experience to champion compassion, visibility, and nuanced storytelling. With a background in pharmacy policy, pharmacology, and wellness education, they bridge the worlds of science and creativity with rare clarity and care.

Whether writing propulsive fiction, crafting digital worlds, or tending to the emotional climate of Harperville™, Jack builds spaces where identity, imagination, and humanity can breathe.
